Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and lose if your score goes over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the crystal values.
